Easy Baked Fish Fillets

Description

Looking for a simple fish dinner? Skip the frozen fillets and try this easy recipe for baked, fresh fish fillets. With little prep involved, this is a great last-minute, easy dinner for the family. You can use any firm white fish: cod, haddock, or grouper work well. The breading is not too overwhelming but adds a delicious crunch. Serve it with leafy greens or thinly sliced oven-baked potatoes.

Details

Cooking Time 20 mins
Servings 4
Calories 223
Carbs 5.3g
Fats 7.5g
Protein 33.6g

Ingredients

  • 1 ½ pounds white fish fillets
  • Lime juice
  • ½ cup fresh breadcrumbs
  • 2 tablespoons fresh chopped parsley
  • 1 tablespoon light mayonnaise
  • 1 ½ tablespoons melted butter
  • Black pepper

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 425F.
  2. Place fish in a baking dish coated with cooking spray.
  3. Combine lime juice, mayonnaise, pepper in a small bowl.
  4. Spread mixture over fish.
  5. Sprinkle with breadcrumbs and drizzle with butter.
  6. Bake for 20 minutes or until fish flakes easily when tested with a fork.
  7. Sprinkle with parsley.
  8. Serve!
