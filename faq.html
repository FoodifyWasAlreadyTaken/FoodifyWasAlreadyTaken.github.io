FAQ

How does Greenb00k come up with the recipes?

Greenb00k automatically generates these recipes by filtering through thousands of recipe blogs to find the best ones for you, alongside user-submitted recipes.

Are there recipes available to people with health issues and/or certain restrictions?

Greenb00k offers various filters such as gluten free, low sugar, lactose free and many more to ensure that everyone finds a recipe that is the most suitable to their health.

What if a recipe has not been created yet?

There is an entire feature on our website dedicated to posting your own recipes. You can select ingredients and upload a picture of the recipe.

What if I cannot find an ingredient?

Simply request to add an ingredient in “add a recipe” and fill in the required information. The review team will assess the submission and add the ingredients.

Is there a way to save recipes?

Yes! Create an account to save recipes you would like to cook. There will be an option beside the recipes to save them if you wish to do so. You can view these recipes in your profile tab.

Why do I need to enter the ingredients’ expiry dates?

One of our most important values is reducing food waste. This is the reason why our website allows you to enter the expiry dates of your ingredients. By doing so, we can prioritize those that will expire the quickest.

How can I contact Greenb00k?

To contact Greenb00k, you can send an email to one of the founders. You can find our contact information in the “About us” section.

Does Greenb00k feature sponsored content?

Greenb00k will feature partnered stores and cookbooks in recipes and in advertisements, as we need to make money (or at least break even). You can learn more on our pricing page
