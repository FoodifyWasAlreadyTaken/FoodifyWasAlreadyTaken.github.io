Simple fish dish

Description

Try this easy recipe for fish topped with a cheesy sauce and crispy cro√ªtons. Serve with broccoli.

Cooking Time 10 to 30 mins
Servings 1-2

Ingredients

  • 1 fillet white fish
  • 6 cherry tomatoes quartered
  • 2 tablespoons peas
  • 1 spring onion sliced
  • 50g cream cheese
  • 25g melted butter
  • 1 slice of stale bread, torn into chunks
  • Vegetable oil
  • 1 teaspoon flour
  • 125mL milk

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 390F.
  2. Grease a small ovenproof dish with oil.
  3. Mix the melted butter and bread together in a bowl.
  4. Mix the cream cheese and flour together in another bowl, slowly mix in the milk until smooth, stir in the spring onion and peas.
  5. Place the fish on the greased dish, topped with cream cheese mixture, the tomatoes and finally the bread mixture.
  6. Bake in the oven for 20-25 minutes, or until the fish is cooked through.
  7. Serve!
