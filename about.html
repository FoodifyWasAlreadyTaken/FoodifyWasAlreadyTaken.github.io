Greenb00k: 0 hassle, 0 waste!

Here at Greenb00k, we hope to bring you the best experience you can get for learning new and exciting recipes as fast and conveniently as possible!

Our innovative search engine and database tagging system lets you bring up hundreds of recipes based on the ingredients and appliances you have at home, making the art of cooking as simple and hassle free as possible!

Any user can submit recipes and get feedback from others, and clicking your profile page lets you see your liked recipes and any of the recipes you've decided to share with us!
