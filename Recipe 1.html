Baked Fish with tomatoes, olives and capers

Description

Serve up dinner in 15 minutes with this simple and satisfying baked fish with olives, tomato & capers

Details

Cooking Time 15 mins
Servings 4
Calories 275
Carbs 7.9g
Fats 10.6g
Protein 35.4g

Ingredients

  • 4 x 175g fish fillets
  • 250g cherry tomatoes halved
  • 2 tablespoons capers, rinsed and drained
  • 100g pitted olives
  • 1-2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 cups mixed salad leaves

Directions

  1. Preheat oven to 390F.
  2. Place fish in a roasting pan, scatter with the tomatoes, olives and capers, then drizzle with olive oil.
  3. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Bake for 15 minutes.
  5. Remove pan from oven, cover with foil and leave it to rest for 5 minutes.
  6. Divide the fish, tomatoes, olives and capers among 4 plates.
  7. Stir the vinegar into the pan juices and spoon over the fish.
  8. Serve with salad leaves!
