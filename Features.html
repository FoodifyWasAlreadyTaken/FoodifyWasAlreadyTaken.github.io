Why Greenb00k?

    Save Time!

  • Greenb00k finds recipes with ingredients you already have, so you can make yourself a delicious meal without having to go back to the grocery store.

    • Save Money!

  • When you don't have enough ingredients to make an entire meal, Greenb00k offers you to buy the missing ingredients at a store near you at a discounted price, because they will soon be out of date.

    • Save The Planet!

  • By making Greenb00k recipes, you're reducing food waste in your house and in your city's grocery stores

So far, Greenb00k users have saved more than $ and tons of food !
